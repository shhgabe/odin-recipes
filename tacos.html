Tacos Recipe

Description

Wonderful authentic tacos I first tasted in Guatemala at a taco stand. May be a little different to the American taste, but the green sauce is the crowning touch. The flavors are the subtle flavors of Central and South America.

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes, cubed
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tomatillos, husked and quartered
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 1 jalapeno chile pepper, diced
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste/li>
  • 3 avocados, halved with pits removed
  • 10 (6 inch) corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large saucepan, and fill with enough water to cover.
  2. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain.
  3. Crumble the ground beef into a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook for a few minutes, then stir in the onion.
  5. Cook until meat is no longer pink, and the onion is tender.
  6. Drain excess grease, and gently stir in the potatoes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Place the tomatillos, garlic, jalapeno, cilantro,and peeled avocados into a blender. Process until smooth.
  9. Heat tortillas in a skillet until warm and flexible.
  10. Spoon on some of the meat mixture, and top with the sauce.
  11. Roll up, and enjoy.
