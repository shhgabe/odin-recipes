Tito's American Mule Recipe

Description

Spicy ginger and fresh lime give this effervescent cocktail a kick you can enjoy anytime. Doesn't hurt that this one's traditionally served in a copper mug, which just so happens to pair well with our bottle.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
  • 3 ounces ginger beer
  • ½ ounce freshly squeezed lime juice

Garnish

Lime wedge or wheel

Steps

  1. Fill a chilled copper mug with ice.
  2. Add Tito's Handmade Vodka.
  3. Add fresh lime juice.
  4. Add ginger beer.
  5. Garnish with lime wedge or wheel.
